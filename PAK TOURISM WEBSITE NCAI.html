This stunning valley in the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan is something straight out of a fairy tale. NARAN-KAGHAN

Naran & Kaghan are the two magnificent valleys located in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan and around 285 kilometers from the Capital, Islamabad. Abbottabad and Mansehra are the two major cities down the way to Naran Kaghan.
